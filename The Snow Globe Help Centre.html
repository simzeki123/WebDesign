Hot Questions

How Can I Track My Order Shipping Status?

When a product is purchase ,the tracking number of the product will be mail to you, shipping status can be view under the order tracking page.

How Do I rate The product I purchased?

After your order has been completed,a email will be sent and you will be able to review the product with the link given

What Is The Delivery Time Of My Order?

Courier delivery time usually depends on where you are staying.It usally takes less than 1 week for your product to arrive.Customer able to see the exact range when the product will be delivered by using the tracking number given.


How Do I Log In To My Membership Account?

If you already have a member account in our website,then theres a login option in the top right corner of our website.

What Should I Do If I Forget My Password?

Theres a forget password option below the login option top right corner of the website.When click it will prompt user to enter email for password recovery

How Do I Change My Information At Membership Account?

Theres a memeber profile page which allows the user to edit their details.


How Is The Transaction Fee Calculated?

Transaction fee will be calculated as 2% of the final amount paid by buyer for a particular order, inclusive of Shipping fee. It will be rounded up to 2 decimal places.Do note that transaction fee is subjected to SST of 6%

Does All The Product Have Shipping Fee?

Not all the product have shipping fee,the product that don't have shipping fee will be stated in the product desccription page.

Is There Any Additional Fee That Need To Be Pay When Receiving Product?

No addtional fee is required to be pay when receiving the product unless it's a COD payement.


How Can I Get Refunded For Cancelled Product?

When a product is cancle,you will be automatically be refund by our system

Why is my order automatically cancelled?

If the order is cancle the product is out of stock or the address given is out of reach.

How Can I Return The Product That I Purchase

Product purchase are only eligible for return if it did'nt exceed 1 week from the received date.To return the product please contact customer service for more info.
